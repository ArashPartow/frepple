Overall introduction

FrePPLe is an advanced planning and scheduling tool for your supply chain.

FrePPLe manages your planning process from sales forecasting, inventory planning, purchasing and production planning. All integrated in a single application.

Frustrated with your Excel spreadsheets? Explore frePPLe to boost your planning process to the next level!
